Karen Baptist Churches USA Brief History

The Karen Baptist Leaders met the first time and discussed about the dream to form a Karen Baptist organization in the United States on October 22-23, 2008 in Dallas Texas.

There was a second meeting that took place through a telephone conference on April 28, 2009 and 32 participants joined the telephone conference meeting. We all agreed and decided to form Karen Baptist Churches USA and assigned

  1. Rev. Tha Hgay as Chairperson
  2. Rev. Hsa Moo as Vice Chairperson
  3. Rev. Saw Ler Htoo as Secretary
  4. Thramu Aye Aye Thaw as Secretary 2

The third meeting was held on June 26, 2009 in Pasadena, CA and we discussed and decided to form KBCUSA with seven regions.

  1. East Coast South
  2. East Coast North
  3. Midwest South
  4. Midwest North
  5. Central South
  6. Central North
  7. West Coast

KBCUSA First Mass Meeting was held on July 22-23, 2009 in Fort Wyne, IN and the meeting assigned the Board of KBCUSA as follows:
